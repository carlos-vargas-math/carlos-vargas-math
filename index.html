Welcome to Carlos Vargas' personal webpage

I am interested in free and (operator-valued) non-commutative probability, large random matrices, boolean probability, incidence algebras, cumulants and statistics, Moebius functions, Matroids, large random graphs and topological spaces, geometrical and topological data analysis, distributions on manifolds.
